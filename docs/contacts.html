DevOpera is an AI-powered solution, streamlines DevOps workflows for Engineers and SREs using Large Language Models to auto-generate config files for Ansible, Kubernetes, Jenkins, and more. Engage with DevOpera via an intuitive chat interface, discuss DevOps strategies, and receive tool/configuration recommendations. Finally, let DevOpera generate the configs you need for seamless deployments!

DevOpera streamlines workflows, automates mundane tasks, and reduces the need for hiring additional specialized DevOps engineers, ultimately saving you time and resources.

We offer special discounted rates for partner organizations and teams participating in our beta program. Please email us at beta@devopera.ai to learn more.

DevOpera is designed to work with a wide range of DevOps tools, including Kubernetes, Jenkins, Terraform, Docker, and many more. For Enterprises, DevOpera can even work with their proprietary tools. DevOpera can generate a variety of configurations such as Ansible playbooks, Docker Compose files, CI/CD pipelines, and other tool-specific configuration files.
